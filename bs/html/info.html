Gameplay

Sink all the pirate ships by shooting squares on the board with the cannon
The numbers at the top and sides tall you how many ships are in that column/row
The numbers on the board tell you how many Pirate Ships are next to them

Power-ups

Safe marker lets you block a square on the grid
The barage will blow up ships in the row and column it is used on
The spyglass reveals 5 random tiles without ships
Wind will blow away all the clouds in a 3x3 area
